# Data Deletion Instructions We take your privacy seriously. To delete your data from our app, follow these steps: ## Steps to Delete Your Account: 1. Open the app and go to your **Settings**. 2. Navigate to **Privacy** > **Account Management**. 3. Click on **Delete Account**. 4. Follow the on-screen instructions to confirm your account deletion. Your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted. ## Contact Us If you encounter any issues or need further assistance with data deletion, please reach out to us at [support@example.com](mailto:support@example.com).
